What is theScore?

History

theScore is a media company based in Toronto, Ontario, it began as Sportscope, a tv show in Canada displaying sports scores, news, and sports betting information.

In the 2000s, it expanded its reach to air live coverage of many different sports as theScore Television Network.

Later on, during the smartphone boom, in 2007, it developed its own first media app which would prove to be a huge success and led to the creation of its sports betting app later down the line in 2019.

Products

TheScoreMedia app focuses on the delivery of sports and news for a wide variety of sports teams, providing articles, tweets, videos and real time information about events.

Meanwhile, theScoreBet enables fans to bet on their favourite teams with a variety of matchups and custom parlays.
